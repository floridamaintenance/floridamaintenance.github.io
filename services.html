Pressure Cleaning

Our commercial grade pressure cleaning and scrubbing equipment will clean any stubborn dirt, grime, or oils that are harming your property's appearance and performance. We clean an assorted variety of property and surface types and can tailor our treatment to meet your specific needs, including hot water pressure washing treatments. We can provide hoovering pressure cleaning, striping preperation, road divider cleaning, and dumpster area cleaning. We have the experience and dedication required to make sure your property is in the best condition that it can be.

Sweeping

Our street sweeping service cleans parking lots and parking garages for apartment and office complexes, retail centers, warehouses, as well as sidewalks, roadways, and tunnels, just to list a few of the types of outdoor environments we can clean. If it’s vehicle accessible, there’s a good chance we can clean it with our larger equipment. If it’s a more restricted area, then one of our pressure cleaning options might be a better fit.

Construction Cleanup

Florida Maintenance is your top choice when it comes to your construction cleanup projects. We can handle everything from dust to runoff marks, whether it be a larger more open area, or a smaller area such as a staircase. You can count on us to keep your project spotless and ready to impress your clients.

Graffiti Removal

We offer graffiti removal using state of the art hot water pressure cleaning units. This is the least abrasive method for removing graffiti, so it won’t heavily impact the appearance of the underlying material. Whether it be street graffiti or marking paint, we can get the job done.
