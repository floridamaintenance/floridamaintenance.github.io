Power Sweeping

Florida Maintenance’s power sweeping service can be added to any of our other services or be quoted as its own exclusive service. Our services are available on an on call basis, or a weekly, monthly, bi-annual, etc. basis.

Our street sweeping service cleans parking lots and parking garages for apartment and office complexes, retail centers, warehouses, as well as sidewalks, roadways, and tunnels, just to list a few of the types of outdoor environments we can clean. If it’s vehicle accessible, there’s a good chance we can clean it with our larger equipment. If it’s a more restricted area, then one of our pressure cleaning options might be a better fit.

Our Parking Lot Sweeping Services Will Make Your Property Look Good

Sweeping is definitely the most effective service available to provide a clean appearance to your property.

Sweeping removes large debris and surface dirt from your facility. Our sweeping services cleans parking lots and parking garages for apartment and office complexes, retail centers, warehouses, etc. During the bidding process, we will decide what equipment is needed to leave your facility looking clean and spotless.
