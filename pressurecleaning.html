Pressure Cleaning

Florida Maintenance’s pressure cleaning service can be added to any of our other services, or be quoted as its own exclusive service. Our services are available on an on call basis or a weekly, monthly, bi-annual, etc. basis.

We Clean It All

Whether your project is a 900,000 sq ft parking garage under construction or a flourishing South Florida shopping mall, Florida Maintenance provides a quick response, professional service, and impeccable quality.

Our commercial grade pressure cleaning and scrubbing equipment will clean any stubborn dirt, grime, or oils that are harming your property's appearance and performance. All of our pressure cleaning trucks have 600 or 1,800 gallon water holding tanks for industrial and commercial areas that have no direct access to water hookups. We clean an assorted variety of property and surface types and can tailor our treatment to meet your specific needs, including hot water pressure washing treatments. We can provide hovering pressure cleaning, striping preparation, road divider cleaning, and dumpster area cleaning. We have the experience and dedication required to make sure your property is in the best condition that it can be.

The Right Equipment

• Hot water capabilities up to 260 degrees.

• 3,500 PSI at 9 gallons per minute pumps for efficient cleaning.

• Chemical sprayers on all our equipment.

• Acid washing for removing hard stains from water stains to rust spots in concrete.

• State of the art ride on scrubbers with 5 foot cleaning paths for large open floors.

• Surface cleaners ranging from 18 to 36 inches, and turbo nozzles to clean in between any areas evenly.

• We regularly work from boom lifts.

• Non-Marking hose.

Industry pricing normally ranges from 3 to 8 cents per sq ft, depending on the size and grade of service your project requires.
